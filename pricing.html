Common questions

How is this different from Instrumentl or GrantStation?
Those tools hand you more matches to sort through. Sharke does the work and tells you which grants can actually fund your mission. Most tools rent an AI to guess your matches from a description. Sharke runs a model built to learn from what actually gets funded, checks every grant against funder 990 data and award history, and builds the verified pipeline for you. They add decisions to your week. We remove them.
Can we try it before committing?
First month is free. No contract. Cancel anytime. You’ll see results on your first grant check.
We already check eligibility. Why do we need this?
Eligibility is table stakes. Sharke checks funder behavior: who they’ve actually funded, whether they take cold applicants, whether their priorities have shifted. That’s the stuff guidelines don’t tell you.

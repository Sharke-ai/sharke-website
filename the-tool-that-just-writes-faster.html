The trap

Faster is not funded

AI tools promise speed: 5x faster applications, more grants found, a whole AI team in one tab. But volume is not funding. Writing more applications to grants you cannot fund just loses the same hours, faster. Three things a faster tool does not do:

•
It does not tell you what to pursue
A faster tool writes more applications. It does not read award history to tell you which grants have a documented path to funding for an organization like yours. Selection decides the money, not speed, and that is the part it skips.
•
It does not do the work
Self-serve means you still do it, only faster. For a team already wearing six hats, a faster tool is one more tab to run, not the time you actually need. The hours still have to come from somewhere.
•
It does not learn your funders
A rented prompt gives an opinion from zero every time, with no memory of what actually got funded. It cannot get sharper about the funders who could fund you, because it never learns from how they award.
